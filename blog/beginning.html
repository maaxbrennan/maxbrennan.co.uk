Beginning

1st December 2020

At the end of the day it’s all about happiness I think. Life’s way too short.

Life could be    or it could be    and you wouldn’t even have a clue.

Life in England is getting unhappy. All the news is unhappy. The government is making everyone unhappy. The two lockdowns and the tier system (which was a third lockdown in disguise) has removed any enjoyment and social interaction from the daily routine of everyone. I’ve spoken with people on zoom, even tried a music event livestream, but it’s not the same. I want to be doing the real thing. This isn’t real life. We are not living.

Back in school, I really wanted to work for a web design company. I learned basic HTML and CSS in computing and thought it would be an ideal job. Fast forward a couple of years and I made it happen when I finished college.

Only for a couple of years though. I’d never had an office job before. I didn’t know that they drain you, but not like a physical job. I was turning into a zombie. I’d wake up to that Apple radar alarm. shower, drive to work, work for 8 hours, drive home, eat, sleep, repeat. Apart from the weekends, where I wouldn’t be working! I’d be sitting inside for almost the full day because the country is locked down. I needed to get out of this and out of my comfort zone. The 40 hour week office job lifestyle can wait a few years.

I'd always been interested in cryptocurrency and sat on the sidelines for 2017 but in the second year of my job I went head first into this new world of money. I put a decent amount of money into Chainlink ($LINK) @ £3.30 and within a few weeks I'd seen my investment double.

I handed my resignation in at the end of October and I properly finished working a few days ago. I’ve got a week of nothing before I help my friend George move to the South of France.

Big plan for 2021 is to move to a lively, young city in the UK to find a new job and also meet some people if that’s allowed.

THE WORLD IS CHANGING
