Beginning

1st December 2020

This is a blog post.

Life is way too short.

Life could be at    or it could be at    and you wouldn’t even have a clue.

So you might as well go and try a few different jobs, locations and ideas out.

As of writing this on the 1st of December 2020, life in England is unhappy. All the news is unhappy. The two lockdowns and the tier system (which was a third lockdown in disguise) has removed any enjoyment and social interaction from the daily routine of everyone. I’ve spoken with people on zoom, even tried a music event livestream, but it’s not the same. It isn't the real thing!

Back in school, I wanted to work for a web design company. I learned HTML and CSS in computing and thought it would be an ideal job. I made it happen when I finished college.

I’d never had an office job before. I turned into a bit of a zombie. I’d wake up to that Apple radar alarm. shower, drive to work, work for 8 hours, drive home, eat, sleep, repeat. It became repetitive.

I handed my resignation in at the end of October and I properly finished working a few days ago. I’ve got a week of nothing before I help my friend George move to the South of France.
